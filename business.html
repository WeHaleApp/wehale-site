Pre / post check-ins

Before a session starts, users answer a quick question (e.g. “How stressed do you feel right now?”) on a slider from 0 to 1. After the session ends, they answer the same question again.

  • See immediate change session-by-session (before vs after)
  • Track trends over time at the individual level
  • Optionally configure the question (stress, energy, focus, calm, etc.)

We recommend reporting only aggregated, privacy-safe insights at the team level.
